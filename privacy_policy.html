Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Brainscribe Solutions LLP, Pune, Maharashtra, India ("Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting personal and confidential information. This Policy complies with the Information Technology Act, 2000, SPDI Rules, 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1. Introduction

This policy explains our privacy commitments, legal basis, and general controls for handling information provided by clients, partners, and website users.

2. Data We Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, company details)
  • Financial data (banking and payment details)
  • Technical data (IP address, device info, logs)
  • Client confidential information including proprietary source code

3. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed for service delivery, contract execution, financial processing, regulatory compliance, and security monitoring.

4. International Transfers

Data may be transferred internationally with appropriate safeguards including contractual protections and secure infrastructure controls.

5. Security Measures

We implement encryption, role-based access control, secure repository management, and confidentiality agreements. However, no system is fully secure, and liability is limited to the extent permitted by law.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained for contractual duration, statutory compliance, and dispute resolution purposes.

7. User Rights

Subject to applicable law, individuals may request access, correction, or withdrawal of consent by contacting our compliance team through the Contact Us page.

8. Limitation of Liability

Brainscribe shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Total liability is limited as per contractual terms.

9. Governing Law

This Policy is governed by the laws of India. Courts in Pune, Maharashtra, India shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
