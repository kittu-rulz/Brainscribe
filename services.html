Offshore Outsourcing

Extended Development Teams (XDT) — Offshore Model

The Extended Development Team (XDT) model from Brainscribe gives you the best of both worlds: the cost efficiency of offshore development with the accountability and integration of an in-house team.

Your XDT is a dedicated group of Brainscribe engineers, QAs, designers, and managers who work exclusively on your products — following your processes, using your tools, attending your standups, and functioning as a seamless extension of your organisation.

Team Setup & Onboarding

Rapid team mobilisation within 2–4 weeks — from role definition and hiring to workspace setup, NDAs, and tool provisioning.

Dedicated Project Management

A dedicated PM/Scrum Master bridges your stakeholders and the offshore team, ensuring clear communication and accountability.

Scalable Team Size

Start with 2 engineers and scale to 20+ seamlessly. Resource augmentation or reduction aligned to your project roadmap.

Timezone Flexibility

Flexible working hours with IST overlap for US, UK, Australia, and Middle East clients — 4–8 hours of live collaboration daily.

How XDT Engagement Works

  1. Discovery Call — Define team composition, skills, processes, and tools needed.
  2. Team Mobilisation — Sourcing, interviews, selection, and onboarding within 2–4 weeks.
  3. Integration & Kickoff — Team integrates into your Jira, GitHub, Slack, and daily standups.
  4. Ongoing Delivery — Sprint-based delivery, regular reviews, and transparent reporting.
